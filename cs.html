Commerce Stream

What is Commerce?

Commerce is a field of study that focuses on business, trade, and finance. It involves the study of various economic activities, including the exchange of goods and services, production, distribution, and consumption. The commerce stream provides students with knowledge and skills related to business management, financial accounting, economics, marketing, and entrepreneurship.
