    What is Jewellery designing?

  • This after 10th design course concentrates on the jewellery manufacturing industry and gem identification for both the export and domestic markets.
  • The course curriculum covers subjects like Introduction to jewellery design, diamond grading, colour stone identification, jewellery manufacturing, gemstone rendering, and Shaping.

    • Eligibility: A student must have passed class 10 from a recognised board.

    Duration:The duration of the course is 3 months - 1 year.

    Fees:Rs 25,000 - Rs 1,25,000

    Fashion Desinging Course:

    These fashion design courses after 10th allow students to explore a wide range of art and style disciplines. It prepares them for the most comprehensive and detailed Fashion Design career. The diploma in fashion design courses after 10th teach students about fashion design, retail, and luxury.



    Students from a variety of backgrounds will be able to gain a solid foundation, adapt to the industrial perspective, and make a career after pursuing fashion designing courses after 10th. The curriculum covers topics like drafting and pattern making, garment manufacturing, fashion sketching and illustrations, visual design, CAD, and fabric technology.

    Duration: The duration of the course is 1 - 3 years.

    Interior Designing:

    These interior design courses after 10th are all about planning space, selecting materials, and designing an area creatively and professionally based on design fundamentals. The diploma in interior design course curriculum covers subjects like theory of interior design, art, sketching and drafting, colours, furniture design, AutoCAD, and sketch-up V-ray.

    Duration: The duration of the course is 1 - 3 years.

    Eligibility – A student must have passed class 10 from a recognised board.

    Fees:Rs 49,500 - Rs 1,00,000
