POLYTECHNIC COURSE

Polytechnic is also known as diploma course. The duration of Polytechnic course is 3 years. Students can take admission to Polytechnic course after Class 10th or 12th. In the past many years, a significant increase in the total number of admissions into polytechnic courses has been observed. Many institutes offer a wide range of diploma courses in various fields, including engineering. Several top engineering colleges also offer polytechnic diploma courses in various B.Tech specializations

List of some polytechnic degrees the colleges provide are:

  • Civil Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Chemical Engineering
  • Computer Science and Engineering
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering
  • Electrical and Electronics Engineering
  • Automobile Engineering

    • What is polytechnic?

    A Polytechnic is a diploma or vocational course in which an institute focuses on delivering technical education. Generally, polytechnic courses are a three-year program after completion of which candidates obtain a certificate. Polytechnic also helps students to start their careers by getting a good job at a reputed company. Most of the institutes offer polytechnic diplomas in civil, mechanical, electrical, and newly emerging branches.

    Difference betweem polytechinc and Btech?

    The basic difference between polytechnic and B.Tech is that polytechnic is a diploma course whereas B.Tech is a degree course. Polytechnic courses are generally for three years, whereas B.Tech is a four-year undergraduate program. In terms of fee structure, B.Tech is a high-fee course as compared to polytechnic.
