Arts Stream

what is arts stream?

The Arts stream, also known as Humanities, encompasses a broad range of subjects related to human society, culture, and creative expression. It is an interdisciplinary field that provides insights into the social, cultural, historical, political, and philosophical aspects of human life. This stream is ideal for students with interests in languages, literature, history, psychology, sociology, political science, and other social sciences.

    • Skills Acquired

  • Critical Thinking: Ability to analyze and evaluate ideas and arguments.
  • Research Skills: Conducting research, collecting data, and interpreting results.
  • Communication: Effective verbal and written communication skills.

  • Creative Expression: Skills in creative writing, art, music, and performance.
  • Analytical Skills: Ability to understand complex social, political, and economic issues.

    • The arts stream is an excellent choice for students passionate about understanding human society, culture, and creative expression. It offers a broad and diverse curriculum that can lead to fulfilling careers in education, media, social services, arts, and more. With the right skills and knowledge, students can make significant contributions to society and pursue their passions.
