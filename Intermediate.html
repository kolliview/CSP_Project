INTERMEDIATE COURSE

There are several branches in the intermediate course.It is a 2 years course.

THE BRANCHES IN INTERMEDIATE ARE:

  • MPC(Maths,Physics,Chemistry)
  • BIPC(Biology,Physics,Chemistry)
  • MEC(Maths,Economics,Commerce)
  • HEC(History,Economics,Civics)
  • CEC(Commerce,Economics,Computer Science.)
